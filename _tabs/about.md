## Short Bio

Md. Abde Mannaf is currently serving as a Senior Lecturer in the Department of CSE at Northern University of Bangladesh. His research focuses on nonlinear dynamics, soliton theory, and fractional models in mathematical physics. He has published in internationally reputed journals including *Scientific Reports*, *Chaos, Solitons & Fractals*, and *Alexandria Engineering Journal*.
